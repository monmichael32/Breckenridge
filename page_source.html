What is Bonus Time?
Bonus Time is inventory that is offered at deeply discounted rates to our owners, their parents, and their children (21 and older). Bonus Time is only available to Club Members and Gold Members.

Why is Bonus Time sometimes unavailable?
Bonus Time is last minute inventory that has not been reserved by another owner or guest, so it can be difficult to secure during high demand times such as weekends and prime travel times. You will see the most Bonus Time availability from Sunday through Thursday nights, as well as in off peak times of the year like spring and fall.

What time do new nights become available for online booking?
At 7:00 a.m. MT, the next available day within your Bonus Time booking window will become available online for booking. 

How often is the Bonus Time calendar updated?
The Bonus Time calendar is linked to live inventory. The Owner Relations department uses this same calendar for all Bonus Time reservation bookings.

GC8 Bonus Time Policies:

  • Bonus Time becomes available once you are inside 14 days of your desired travel date(s).
  • Full payment is taken at the time the reservation is made.
  • Reservations are fully refundable until 48 hours prior to check-in at 4 p.m. Inside 48 hours of check-in at 4 p.m., Bonus Time reservations are fully non-refundable.
